FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a free accident report? +
Click any "Tell Us About Your Accident" button on this site. You'll be taken to a form where you can provide details about your accident. A representative will help obtain your official police report — at no cost to you.
How often is the news feed updated? +
The live feed automatically refreshes every 15 minutes pulling the latest accident news from Google News. Simply reload the page at any time to see the most current reports.
Can I filter news by my state? +
Yes! Use the search bar at the top of the news feed, or click any state name in the sidebar or navigation menu. You can also type any keyword like "highway," "fatal," or a city name to narrow results.
What should I do immediately after a car accident? +
1. Check for injuries and call 911. 2. Move to safety if possible. 3. Exchange insurance and contact information. 4. Document the scene with photos. 5. File a police report. 6. Contact your insurance company. 7. Request a copy of the official police report.
Is this site free to use? +
Yes, IHateCarAccidents.com is completely free to use. All accident news, resources, and guides are available at no charge. The free accident report request service is also provided at no cost to you.
